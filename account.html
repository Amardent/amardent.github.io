Account Deletion Guide

Deleting your account is irreversible and will remove all your data, preferences, and information associated with it. Before proceeding, please review the alternatives and consequences outlined below.

Consequences of Account Deletion

  • All your data and user information will be permanently deleted.
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.

Step-by-Step Deletion Process

  1. Go to Settings > Account Settings.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the "Delete Account" button.
  3. Read the account deletion warning carefully, then confirm deletion.
Important Warning
Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please be certain before proceeding.
